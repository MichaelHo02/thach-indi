Privacy Policy for Time Jar App

Last updated: Dec 23 2024

1. Data collection & Usage

Time Jar does not collect, track, or store any personal data from users. Your privacy is fully respected, and no information is shared with third parties or used for analytics or advertising.

2. Use of Live Activity Feature

Time Jar uses Apple's Live Activity feature to provide real-time updates about your logged activities on the Lock Screen or in Dynamic Island. This functionality operates locally on your device and does not involve the collection, storage, or sharing of any user data.

3. Permission & Access

Any permissions requested by Time Jar, such as notifications, are strictly for enhancing the app's functionality. These permissions are essential for delivering features like reminders and progress updates. No data is saved, collected, or sent outside your device.

4. Account & Personal Information

Time Jar does not require an account or personal information to function. All app features are fully accessible without login or data sharing.
