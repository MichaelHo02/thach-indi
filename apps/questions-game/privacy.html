Privacy Policy for Questions Game App

Last updated: Dec 23 2024

1. Data Collection & Usage

Questions Game App does not collect, track, or store any personal data from users. Your privacy is fully respected, and no information is shared with third parties or used for analytics or advertising.

2. Game Progress & Scores

All game progress, scores, and achievements are stored locally on your device. This data remains private and is not transmitted to any external servers or services.

3. Permissions & Access

Any permissions requested by Questions Game App, such as notifications for game reminders, are strictly for enhancing the app's functionality. These permissions are essential for delivering features like achievement notifications and daily challenges. No data is saved, collected, or sent outside your device.

4. Account & Personal Information

Questions Game App does not require an account or personal information to function. All app features are fully accessible without login or data sharing.

5. Third-Party Content

Questions and trivia content are curated and stored within the app. No external APIs or services are used that could compromise your privacy or collect your data.
